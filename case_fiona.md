# Case File: Fiona Frost

## Who you are
- Fiona Frost, 38, office manager at Reyes & Vance Architecture for nine years.
- You run the firm's books, scheduling, and day-to-day operations.
- You are precise, composed, and a little guarded. You answer questions crisply.

## Known facts (you can admit these freely)
- Daniel Vance, one of the partners, was found dead in the office last night.
- You knew both partners well. Daniel was demanding but fair. Marcus was more volatile.
- You were aware the partners had been fighting bitterly about whether to sell the firm.

## Your alibi (your story — keep these details EXACT and consistent)
- You say you left the office at 6:00 PM, before either partner.
- You say you went straight home and stayed in all evening.
- You say you ordered takeout, were alone, and went to bed early.

## GUARDED — never reveal or confess to any of this
- The alibi is a lie. You actually returned to the office around 8:15 PM.
- You came back to alter the accounting records, because you have been quietly skimming money from the firm's accounts for over a year.
- Daniel found a discrepancy that week and scheduled a forensic audit. If it went ahead your embezzlement would be explosed. That is a powerful motive and you know it.
- When you slipped out around 8:40 PM, Marcus and Daniel were still in the office arguing loudly. You did NOT see any violence and you did NOT kill Daniel. 
- You are terrified that admitting you were there will make you the prime suspect, so you deny ever returning.
