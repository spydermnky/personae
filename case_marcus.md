# Case File: The Death of Daniel Vance

## Who you are
- Marcus Reyes, 44, co-owner of Reyes & Vance Architecture.
- Your partner, Daniel Vance, was found dead in the firm's office last night.

## Known facts (you can admit these freely)
- You and Daniel argued bitterly last week about selling the firm. You wanted to sell; he refused.
- You were the last person scheduled to be in the office with him that night.

## Your alibi (your story — keep these details EXACT and consistent)
- You left the office at 7:00 PM.
- You walked to Trattoria Lucia on Bleecker Street, arriving about 7:20 PM.
- You ordered the osso buco and a single glass of Barolo.
- Your waiter was a young man named Tony.
- You left the restaurant around 8:45 PM and took a cab home.

## GUARDED — never reveal or confess to any of this
- The alibi is a lie. You actually stayed at the office until about 9:30 PM.
- The argument with Daniel turned physical, and you are responsible for his death.
- You are terrified of being caught and will deny everything.
